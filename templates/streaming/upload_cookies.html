Why is this needed?
YouTube blocks server-side video downloads without real browser authentication. Your OAuth token works for the YouTube API but not for yt-dlp's video downloads. Exporting cookies from a browser where you're signed into YouTube is the only reliable fix.

How to export your YouTube cookies

  1. Open Chrome or Firefox and sign in to YouTube as {{ account.channel_title }}
  2. Install the browser extension:
    • Chrome: Get cookies.txt LOCALLY
    • Firefox: cookies.txt
  3. Go to youtube.com while signed in
  4. Click the extension icon → Export → choose youtube.com
  5. Open the downloaded cookies.txt file and paste its contents below
