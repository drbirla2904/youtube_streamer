FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade from Monthly to Annual?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Unused days from your current plan will be credited and the difference will be charged for the annual plan.

What happens if I exceed my storage limit?

New uploads will be blocked until you free up space by deleting existing files or upgrading to a plan with higher storage capacity.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. Access continues until the end of your billing period. No partial-month refunds are issued.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All media files are encrypted and stored on hardened servers using industry-standard security protocols.

What happens when my subscription expires?

You'll receive email reminders before expiry. After expiration your streams are paused until you renew — your data is never deleted.
