Who we are

A platform built around your YouTube channel

StreamLoop is a YouTube playlist streaming automation platform built for creators, educators, musicians, and brands who want their channel live around the clock — without managing servers, encoders, or command-line tools.

We connect directly to your YouTube account via OAuth, pull your playlist videos on the fly using yt-dlp (no downloads or storage required), and broadcast them as a live stream to your channel in a seamless loop.

Our mission

Streaming infrastructure should be invisible. Our goal is to hide all complexity behind a clean dashboard so you can go live in minutes — not hours.

From handling live RTMP output to managing retries and playlist loops, StreamLoop keeps running reliably even when you are offline.

What you can do

  • Connect your YouTube channel securely via Google OAuth.
  • Pick any YouTube playlist and stream it live instantly.
  • Loop the playlist 24/7 with a single toggle.
  • Monitor all your active streams from one dashboard.
  • Start, stop, and manage multiple streams per account.
What we stand for
Secure by default

YouTube OAuth 2.0 — we never store your Google password. Your channel stays in your control.

Zero friction

No encoding software, no downloads, no server setup. Connect, pick a playlist, press Start.

Always on

Loop mode keeps your playlist repeating indefinitely — perfect for lo-fi, devotional, and ambient streams.
