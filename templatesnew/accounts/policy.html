This policy describes how StreamLoop handles your data when you use our YouTube playlist live-streaming service. By using StreamLoop, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

When you use StreamLoop, we may collect the following:

  • Account details — your username, email address, and hashed password.
  • Google OAuth tokens — used solely to create and manage live streams on your YouTube channel on your behalf.
  • YouTube channel information — channel name and ID needed to target the correct live stream destination.
  • Stream metadata — stream names, playlist selections, start/stop times, and loop settings you configure.
  • Usage data — pages visited, actions taken in the dashboard, and device/browser information for debugging and improvement.

How we use your information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and operate your StreamLoop account and streaming service.
  • Authenticate with YouTube on your behalf to create and manage live broadcasts.
  • Stream your selected YouTube playlist via yt-dlp without storing video files.
  • Send account-related emails such as security alerts and support responses.
  • Monitor service performance, diagnose errors, and improve platform reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

YouTube & OAuth data

StreamLoop uses Google OAuth 2.0 to connect to your YouTube channel. We request only the minimum permissions required to create and manage live streams. We do not store your Google password.

OAuth tokens are stored securely and used exclusively to interact with the YouTube Live Streaming API on your behalf. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

StreamLoop's use of YouTube data is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

Cookies

StreamLoop uses essential cookies to keep you signed in and maintain your session. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling session cookies will prevent you from staying logged in.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google / YouTube — via OAuth API calls required to operate the service.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — that host our servers and databases.
  • Monitoring tools — used to track uptime, errors, and performance.
  • Legal authorities — when required by applicable law or legal process.

Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Stream logs and metadata are kept for a limited period for debugging purposes. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

OAuth tokens are deleted when you disconnect your YouTube account or delete your StreamLoop account.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Revoke OAuth access to your YouTube channel at any time.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@streamloop.in.

Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and secure OAuth token storage to protect your information. No online service can guarantee perfect security — please use a strong, unique password for your account.

Children

StreamLoop is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will be revised when changes are posted. Continued use of StreamLoop after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: support@streamloop.in
